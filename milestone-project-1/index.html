Team kiger of frejes

Internationell breeder of Kiger Mustang - is run by Jessica Furman Monasdotter
Currently the breeding consists of a total of ten breeding mares and two stalloins. This action takes place at our farm in peaceful Tängslinge, Gnesta, only an hour from Stockholm. With her well thought through breedingprogram she sells to a big variety of European countries like Norway, Finland and Germany just to mention a few. As a buyer of foals from here you can be very confident that you'll get the best foal possible. Jessica is very caring about her horses prosperous, supriceing to many can be that shes letting her stallion run freely with the mares and it works.
She ensures that the need for each and and every individuals need are met and ofcourse she puts the social interaction into mind too.

Jessica started this journey and built thius dream in her soul already as a little child while reading the Swedish magazine "My horse". One of those days, right in the middle of bulding her own jumping track the dream of owning an own Mustang was born. Then, a few years later, 2010 her dream startet to form in what you'll se here today.
